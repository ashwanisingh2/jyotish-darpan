Astrology Study Guide

☀️ Planets Guide (Navagraha Meanings)

In Vedic Astrology, 9 celestial bodies (Navagrahas) shape our karma. The Sun (Surya) represents the soul and career. The Moon (Chandra) represents the mind and emotions. Mars (Mangal) drives courage and energy. Mercury (Budha) rules communication and business. Jupiter (Guru) is the planet of wisdom and luck. Venus (Shukra) governs relationship harmony. Saturn (Shani) demands hard work and discipline. Rahu brings desires/illusions, and Ketu brings liberation.

🏠 Houses Guide (12 Bhavas of Life)

Your birth chart is divided into 12 houses (Bhavas). 1st House (Lagna): Identity and body. 2nd House: Wealth, family, speech. 3rd House: Courage, short travels, siblings. 4th House: Mother, happiness, home. 5th House: Creativity, children, past-life merits. 6th House: Health, enemies, debts. 7th House: Partnerships and marriage. 8th House: Secrets, longevity, hurdles. 9th House: Religion, foreign travel, higher education. 10th House: Career, fame, actions. 11th House: Income and gains. 12th House: Spiritual liberation, losses, isolation.

❓ FAQ - Astrology Common Questions

Q: What is Ayanamsa?
A: Ayanamsa is the astronomical offset between Tropical (Western) and Sidereal (Vedic) zodiac structures. Jyotish Darpan uses the Lahiri Ayanamsa.

Q: Is my data safe?
A: Yes. All profile parameters (name, place, birth date) are saved locally inside your browser's localStorage database and never shared on external servers.
